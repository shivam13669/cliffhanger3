Kerala Luxury Tour Package

The Kerala Luxury Tour Package (6 Days / 5 Nights) is designed for travelers who wish to experience the finest of God’s Own Country in absolute comfort and style. This premium holiday package covers Kerala’s most sought-after destinations – the rolling tea gardens of Munnar, the wildlife haven of Thekkady, the serene backwaters of Alleppey, and the cultural charm of Cochin (Kochi) – all while staying in handpicked luxury resorts and houseboats that redefine indulgence.

Your journey begins in Cochin, a vibrant coastal city rich in colonial heritage and culture. From here, you travel to Munnar, where misty mountains, tea plantations, and waterfalls create a picture-perfect retreat. A guided tour will take you to top attractions such as the Eravikulam National Park, Tea Museum, and Mattupetty Dam, allowing you to soak in the beauty of Kerala’s most loved hill station while returning each evening to the comfort of your luxury resort.

From Munnar, the journey continues to Thekkady, home to the renowned Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. A unique boat safari on Periyar Lake offers chances to spot elephants, deer, bison, and exotic birds. Spice plantations, cultural shows like Kathakali, and rejuvenating Ayurvedic spa treatments add to the experience, making Thekkady a highlight for nature and culture lovers alike.

Next, you will head to Alleppey, also known as the “Venice of the East”. Here, a stay on a private luxury houseboat lets you explore the backwaters in true elegance. Glide through palm-fringed canals, picturesque villages, and lush paddy fields, while enjoying freshly prepared Kerala delicacies served onboard. The sunset views over the Vembanad Lake create unforgettable memories.

Finally, the tour concludes in Cochin, where you will discover the charm of Fort Kochi with its Chinese Fishing Nets, colonial churches, spice markets, and the famous Jew Town with its antique shops and Paradesi Synagogue. The city’s mix of history, culture, and modern vibrancy makes it the perfect finale to your Kerala luxury holiday.

This 6 Days / 5 Nights Kerala Luxury Tour Package is perfect for honeymooners, families, and premium travelers seeking a stress-free, comfortable, and indulgent vacation. With luxury accommodations, personalized services, private transfers, and carefully curated experiences, this tour offers not just a trip but a memorable journey through Kerala’s finest landscapes and traditions.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Cochin – Transfer to Munnar

    Arrive at Cochin International Airport / Railway Station, where you will be greeted with a warm welcome by our representative. Begin your journey towards Munnar, one of Kerala’s most picturesque hill stations, known for its endless tea plantations, misty valleys, and cool climate. Enjoy the scenic drive through lush plantations, cascading waterfalls, and rolling hills, with stopovers at Valara and Cheeyappara Waterfalls—perfect for photography. On arrival, check in to your luxury resort in Munnar, surrounded by tea gardens and serene landscapes. Spend the evening at leisure, indulging in resort amenities or simply relaxing in the peaceful environment. Overnight stay at Munnar (Luxury Resort). 👉 Highlights: Scenic drive, Valara & Cheeyappara Waterfalls, misty tea gardens, and luxury resort stay.

  • Day 2: Munnar Sightseeing Tour

    After breakfast, enjoy a full day of sightseeing in Munnar. Start with Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and stunning rolling hills. Continue to the Tea Museum, where you’ll learn about Kerala’s rich tea heritage. Later, visit Mattupetty Dam for a peaceful boating experience, Echo Point for its natural echo phenomenon, and the serene Kundala Lake, surrounded by lush greenery. Return to your resort and relax with luxury comforts. Overnight stay in Munnar (Luxury Resort). 👉 Highlights: Eravikulam National Park, Tea Museum, boating at Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Kundala Lake.

  • Day 3: Munnar – Thekkady (95 km / 3-4 hrs)

    After breakfast, check out and drive to Thekkady, famous for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. On arrival, check in at your luxury jungle resort. In the afternoon, enjoy a unique boat safari on Periyar Lake, where you may spot elephants, deer, wild boars, and exotic birds along the banks. Optional activities include a spice plantation tour, Kalaripayattu martial arts show, Kathakali dance performance, or a rejuvenating Ayurvedic spa. Overnight stay in Thekkady (Luxury Resort). 👉 Highlights: Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, boat safari, spice plantations, cultural shows.

  • Day 4: Thekkady – Alleppey Luxury Houseboat (140 km / 4-5 hrs)

    After breakfast, drive to Alleppey (Alappuzha), the backwater paradise of Kerala. Check into your private luxury houseboat, featuring elegant interiors, modern amenities, and personalized service. Cruise through the serene Vembanad Lake, palm-fringed canals, and traditional villages. Relish freshly prepared Kerala delicacies onboard while soaking in the tranquil surroundings. Witness the magical sunset over the backwaters before retiring for the night on the houseboat. Overnight stay on Luxury Houseboat in Alleppey. 👉 Highlights: Luxury backwater cruise, Vembanad Lake, coconut lagoons, sunset views, authentic Kerala cuisine.

  • Day 5: Alleppey – Cochin (55 km / 2 hrs)

    After breakfast, disembark from the houseboat and drive back to Cochin (Kochi). Begin your city tour with Fort Kochi, where you’ll see the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets, St. Francis Church, and the Dutch Palace (Mattancherry Palace). Continue to Jew Town, famous for its spice markets, antique shops, and the historic Paradesi Synagogue. End your day with a relaxing walk along Marine Drive or enjoy luxury shopping in Kochi. Check in to your premium hotel for a comfortable stay. Overnight stay in Cochin (Luxury Hotel). 👉 Highlights: Fort Kochi heritage sites, Chinese Fishing Nets, Dutch Palace, Paradesi Synagogue, shopping in Kochi.

  • Day 6: Departure from Cochin

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Depending on your flight/train schedule, you may explore last-minute shopping or sightseeing. Later, transfer to Cochin International Airport / Railway Station for your onward journey. 👉 Highlights: Local shopping, comfortable transfer.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) – Kerala Luxury Tour Package

1. What makes this Kerala Luxury Tour different from regular packages?

This package includes handpicked luxury resorts, a private premium houseboat in Alleppey, curated sightseeing experiences, and private transfers for maximum comfort and exclusivity.

2. What type of accommodations are included?

You’ll stay in 5-star luxury resorts in Munnar and Thekkady, a private luxury houseboat in Alleppey, and a premium hotel in Cochin. All properties are carefully selected for comfort, service, and location.

3. Is the luxury houseboat private or shared?

The houseboat provided is private and exclusive, equipped with modern amenities, personalized service, and onboard dining with freshly prepared Kerala cuisine.

4. What is the best time to book this luxury Kerala tour?

The best time is September to March when the weather is cool and pleasant. However, summers (April–June) are ideal for Munnar’s hills, and monsoon (July–August) offers lush greenery and romantic vibes.

5. Are meals included in this tour package?

Yes. Daily breakfast is included at resorts/hotels, while the luxury houseboat stay includes all meals – lunch, evening tea, dinner, and breakfast. Resorts also offer fine dining and à la carte options.

6. Can this itinerary be customized?

Absolutely. This is a flexible luxury package that can be customized with additional destinations like Kumarakom, Kovalam, or Marari Beach, or upgraded with private guided tours, spa sessions, and cultural experiences.

7. Is this luxury tour suitable for honeymooners?

Yes. The exclusive houseboat stay, private transfers, and luxury resorts make this an ideal honeymoon package in Kerala. Personalized services and romantic settings enhance the experience.

8. How do I reach Cochin to start the tour?

The nearest entry point is Cochin International Airport (COK) or Ernakulam Railway Station, where our representative will welcome you for a seamless start to your journey.

Frequently Ask Questions: India Tours

What are the best times to visit India?

The best times to visit India generally fall between October to March, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant for traveling. However, the ideal time may vary based on the region you plan to visit.

What modes of transportation are available for traveling within India?

India offers various modes of transportation, including domestic flights, trains, buses, and taxis. The Indian Railways is a popular choice for long distances, while local buses and auto-rickshaws are commonly used for short trips within cities.

Is it safe to travel in India?

Traveling in India is generally safe for tourists. However, it is advisable to stay vigilant, follow local guidelines, and take standard safety precautions, such as avoiding isolated areas at night and securing your belongings.

How can I book train tickets in India?

Train tickets can be booked online through the Indian Railways website or through various travel booking platforms. It is recommended to book tickets in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.

What is the best way to experience Indian cuisine while traveling?

To fully experience Indian cuisine, consider dining at local restaurants, trying street food, and participating in cooking classes. Each region has its unique dishes, so be adventurous and explore the local flavors.

Should I hire a guide for my tours in India?

Hiring a local guide can enhance your travel experience by providing insights into local culture, history, and attractions. It is especially beneficial in cities with rich heritage and complex histories.

What cultural etiquettes should I be aware of while traveling in India?

It is important to respect local customs and traditions. Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites, and be mindful of local practices, such as removing shoes before entering homes or temples.

Are there any specific health precautions I should take before visiting India?

Before traveling to India, it is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional regarding vaccinations and health precautions. Carry a basic first aid kit and be cautious with drinking water and street food.

Can I use credit cards in India?

Credit cards are widely accepted in urban areas and major tourist destinations, but it is advisable to carry cash for small purchases and in rural areas. ATMs are available in most cities.

What should I pack for my trip to India?

Pack lightweight, breathable clothing suitable for the climate, comfortable shoes for walking, and a travel adapter for electronics. Also, consider bringing a reusable water bottle and a basic first aid kit.

Are there any restrictions on photography in India?

While photography is generally allowed, certain places, particularly religious sites and military installations, may have restrictions. Always ask for permission when photographing people and be respectful of local customs.

What are some must-visit destinations in India?

India is home to numerous must-visit destinations, including the Taj Mahal in Agra, Jaipur's palaces, Kerala's backwaters, the beaches of Goa, and the spiritual city of Varanasi and Dharamshala. Each region offers unique experiences and attractions.
